Rules for solving a triangle using this script:
  • Only floating-point decimal quantities can be read--no expressions.
  • Insufficient information might be treated as conflicting or invalid.
  • Conflicting or contradictory information results in undefined behavior.
  • The perimeter is sufficient if at least two side lengths are known.
  • For a right triangle, the area and one leg length is sufficient.
  • Knowing nothing but all three angle measures is never sufficient.
Currently unsupported till the near future:
  • Advanced trigonometry for one-angle-two-sides or one-side-two-angles.
  • Solve regular triangles when only the perimeter or area are known.
  • Isosceles triangle rules for filling in more information.
  • Use the area of a right triangle and its hypotenuse, not a leg length.
